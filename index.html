About Me

Michael is a speciality blind manufacturer, a web developer in training, a tech enthusiast, and an avid airsofter. Before setting himself some real-life goals to work towards and meeting the love of his love Dawn, Michael was just blunding through life not focusing on anything but himself living everyday as it comes. Now Michael has set down some real goals to work towards and he is doing his upmost to keep on track.

Goals

When you reach a certian age in your life it is best to set yourself realistic and realable goals. In doing so you can focus your life into a more meaningful journey. Below are the goals Michael has set for himself and what steps he is taking to reach this goals.

Healthy Lifestyle

Michael will achieve a healthy lifestyle by taking steps such as going to the gym three days a week, eating a more nutrient-dense diet with an array of different protein sources and getting more involved in hobbies with a foundation of cardio exercise like Airsoft.

Self Improvement

Michael is taking the time to invest in himself by enrolling in courses studying social media marketing and web development which will improve his work capability in his current role at northavon blinds and, in turn, improve his job prospects for the future.

Financially Stable

Michael is trying to be financially stable in his life by removing any unneeded expenses and by saving money each month from his wages. With his savings, Michael hopes to invest in the stock market, cryptocurrencies, NFT's and real estate.

Happily Every After

Michael has been lucky enough to meet the love of his life at a young age, Dawn. They have been together for over ten crazy eventful years that they have weathered together. This year they will be planning their wedding and starting a family together.
